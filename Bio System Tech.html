Sri Lankan A/L Bio System Technology Stream


Subjects in the Bio System Technology


The Bio System Technology stream in Sri Lanka is an academic pathway offered at the Advanced Level (A/L) in secondary education, tailored for students with a keen interest in biological systems and technological applications in agriculture, environmental management, and related fields. The core subjects in this stream include:

  • Bio System Technology
  • Science for Technology

Students are required to study at least two of the core subjects mentioned above. The third subject can be selected from the following options:

  • Accounting
  • Mathematics
  • Home Economics
  • English
  • Geography
  • Communication and Media Studies
  • Information & Communication Technology
  • Art
  • Business Studies
  • Agricultural Science

The Bio System Technology stream is designed for students aiming to pursue careers in agriculture, environmental science, biotechnology, and other fields related to the management and improvement of biological systems. It provides a strong foundation in both biological sciences and technology, equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to address challenges in these fields.

This stream emphasizes the integration of biological science and technology, focusing on sustainable practices and innovations that improve agricultural productivity, environmental conservation, and resource management. By studying subjects such as Bio System Technology and Science for Technology, students gain practical experience and theoretical knowledge that prepare them for further education and careers in these vital areas.

The Bio System Technology stream is an excellent choice for students passionate about biology and technology, offering numerous opportunities for further education and professional development in these interconnected fields.

1.Biosystems Technology (BST)

The Biosystems Technology (BST) stream focuses on the application of biological and systems engineering principles to address challenges in agriculture, biotechnology, and environmental sustainability. This stream prepares students for careers in industries related to biotechnology, environmental management, and agricultural technology. The following universities offer programs in Biosystems Technology:

  • University of Sri Jayewardenepura
  • University of Jaffna
  • Eastern University, Sri Lanka
  • Rajarata University of Sri Lanka
  • South Eastern University of Sri Lanka
  • Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka
  • Wayamba University of Sri Lanka
  • University of Colombo
  • Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka
  • University of Ruhuna

2.Information Communication Technology (ICT)

The Information Communication Technology (ICT) stream emphasizes the study of technology used for managing and processing information. This stream covers various aspects of computer science, information systems, and communication technologies, preparing students for careers in IT, software development, and digital communication. The following universities offer programs in Information Communication Technology:

  • University of Sri Jayewardenepura
  • University of Kelaniya
  • University of Ruhuna
  • Rajarata University of Sri Lanka
  • South Eastern University of Sri Lanka
  • University of Vavuniya, Sri Lanka
  • University of Colombo
  • Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka

Admission and Academic Focus

Admission to the programs listed above is typically based on academic merit, with students selected according to their examination results and academic performance. Both Biosystems Technology and Information Communication Technology streams offer comprehensive training in their respective fields, equipping students with the skills needed for successful careers in biotechnology, environmental management, information technology, and communication technologies.
