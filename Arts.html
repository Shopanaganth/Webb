Sri Lankan A/L Arts Stream

Subjects in the Arts Stream

The Arts stream in Sri Lankan Advanced Level (A/L) offers a wide range of subjects that students can choose from. These subjects include:

Basket 01 - Social Sciences / Applied Social Studies:

  1. Economics
  2. Geography
  3. History
  4. Home Economics
  5. Agricultural Science / Mathematics / Combined Mathematics
  6. Communication & Media Studies
  7. Information & Communication Technology
  8. Accounting / Business Statistics
  9. Political Science
  10. Logic & Scientific Method
  11. One subject from Technological Subjects:
    • Civil Technology
    • Electrical, Electronic and Information Technology
    • Agro Technology
    • Mechanical Technology
    • Food Technology
    • Bio-Resource Technology

There are three exceptions to this rule:

  1. Students selecting three National Languages; Sinhala, Tamil, and English from Basket 04 (Language Basket) as the three subjects, need not select any subject from Basket 01.
  2. Students selecting a combination of National and Classical languages need not select any subject from Basket 01.

    National Languages are:

    • Sinhala
    • Tamil
    • English

    Classical Languages are:

    • Arabic
    • Pali
    • Sanskrit

    For example, students can select three subjects like Sinhala, Pali, and Sanskrit, etc. These students need not select any subject from Basket 01. However, no candidate can select three classical languages as three subjects. They can select a maximum of two classical languages only.

  3. Students selecting two languages from Basket 04 and the third subject from Religions and Civilizations or Aesthetic subject classified under Basket 02 and 03 given below need not select any subject from Basket 01.

Basket 02 - Religions and Civilizations:

  1. Buddhism
  2. Hinduism
  3. Christianity
  4. Islam
  5. Buddhist Civilization
  6. Hindu Civilization
  7. Christian Civilization
  8. Islamic Civilization
  9. Greek & Roman Civilization

Guidance for selection of subjects from Basket 02:

  • A candidate can select a maximum of two subjects from Basket 02.
  • However, if a religion (e.g., Buddhism) is selected as a subject, the related civilization of the selected religion (e.g., Buddhist Civilization) cannot be offered as another subject from this basket.

Basket 03 - Aesthetic Studies:

Aesthetic subjects consist of four subject areas. These are:

  1. Art
  2. Dancing
  3. Music
  4. Drama & Theatre

This can be further sub-classified as:

  • Art
  • Dancing
    • Sinhala
    • Baratha
  • Music
    • Oriental
    • Carnatic
    • Western
  • Drama & Theatre
    • Sinhala
    • Tamil
    • English

Guidance for selection of subjects from Basket 03:

  • Students can select two subjects out of the above four subject areas. However, no student is allowed to select two subjects from one subject area.

Basket 04 - Languages:

The language basket has three subject areas. These are:

  • National Languages
  • Classical Languages
  • Foreign Languages

National Languages are:

  • Sinhala
  • Tamil
  • English

Classical Languages are:

  • Arabic
  • Pali
  • Sanskrit

Foreign Languages are:

  • Chinese
  • French
  • German
  • Hindi
  • Japanese
  • Malay
  • Russian
  • Korean

Admission and Academic Focus

Admission to the programs listed above is typically based on an All-Island Merit basis, meaning that students are selected based on their academic performance and examination results. The Arts stream is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education, promoting critical thinking, cultural awareness, and effective communication skills.

By choosing from a variety of programs and specializations, students can tailor their education to their interests and career goals, whether in the traditional arts, media, social sciences, or specialized creative fields.
